Information We Collect

This Privacy Policy describes how Dumka Personal AI Journal ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our voice-powered journaling application.

We collect voice recordings, transcribed text, and AI-generated insights from your journal entries to provide personalized experiences.

How We Use Your Information

Your journal entries and voice data are used to provide AI-powered insights, maintain your personal memory system, and improve conversation quality. All processing happens securely with industry-standard encryption.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal journal entries or voice data with third parties. Your thoughts remain private and are used solely to enhance your personal AI experience.

Data Security

We implement end-to-end encryption and secure cloud storage through Firebase to protect your personal journal content against unauthorized access.

Voice Data Processing

Voice recordings are transcribed using advanced AI technology and stored securely. You can delete your data at any time from within the app.
